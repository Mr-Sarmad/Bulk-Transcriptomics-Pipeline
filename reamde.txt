## ğŸ“ Configuration

The pipeline is controlled through `config.yaml`:

```yaml
samples:
  - HS01
  - HS02
  - HS03
  - HS04
  - HS05
  - CL08
  - CL10
  - CL11
  - CL12
  - CL13

reference:
  fasta: refs/genome.fa
  gtf: refs/annotation.gtf

design: studydesign.txt
```

The experimental design (`studydesign.txt`) should look like:

```text
sample	condition
HS01	Treated
HS02	Treated
HS03	Treated
HS04	Control
HS05	Control
CL08	Control
CL10	Control
CL11	Control
CL12	Control
CL13	Control
```
